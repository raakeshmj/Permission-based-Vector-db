Intern Onboarding and General Company Policies

Welcome to the company. This document provides an overview of general policies
and guidelines that all interns should be aware of during their time here.

Working Hours:
Interns are expected to work between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
Any deviation must be approved by the assigned mentor.

Code of Conduct:
All interns must maintain professional behavior, respect colleagues,
and comply with company ethics policies. Harassment, misuse of company
resources, or disclosure of confidential information is strictly prohibited.

IT Usage Policy:
Company-provided laptops and accounts are intended solely for work-related
activities. Installing unauthorized software or accessing restricted systems
is not allowed.

Leave Policy:
Interns are entitled to a limited number of leave days during the internship
period. Leave requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least
two days in advance.

Learning and Evaluation:
Interns are encouraged to ask questions, participate in team meetings,
and complete assigned tasks on time. Performance evaluations are conducted
at the end of the internship based on learning progress and contribution.
